### 2026-07-03 — the magic stays small and edible

The only magic in this story is moon-sugar meeting the Moon Oven; no spells,
no talking Moon, no chosen one.

**Why:** Keeps the stakes bun-sized and the wonder concrete — a kid can hold
one glowing jar in their head.

**Alternatives considered:** A magical Moon spirit who fixes the shortage
(rejected: solves Nova's problem for her); a whole magical bakery (rejected:
too much to explain per page).

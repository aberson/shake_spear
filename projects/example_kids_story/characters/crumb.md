# Character

## Short description

A squat rescue-robot Nova found in the crater scrapyard, rebuilt with an oven
mitt for a left hand. Sorts things when anxious. Beeps in questions.

## Want

To be useful to Nova in exactly the right way at exactly the right moment.

## Fear

Being put back in a drawer. (He was in a drawer a long time.)

## Contradiction

Built for emergencies, terrified of surprises.

## Secret

He keeps one crumb from every batch Nova has ever baked, filed by date, in a
tin behind his chest panel.

## Voice notes

Speaks in short declaratives plus a rising beep for questions: "Shipment
late. Beep? Sugar level: one jar." Never uses ten words where three will do.

## Physical anchors

Oven-mitt hand; a dent shaped like a star on his dome; wheels that squeak on
the left turn only.

## Relationships

Nova — the person who wound him back up when nobody else would.

## Recurring behaviors

Alphabetizes the spice shelf when worried. Warms his mitt on the oven door
before holding Nova's hand.

## Arc

From "useful means following the manual" to "useful means noticing what Nova
needs before she says it."

## Established facts

- Found in the crater scrapyard; rebuilt by Nova.
- His chest tin holds one crumb per batch, filed by date.

## Open questions

- What was Crumb built to rescue, originally?

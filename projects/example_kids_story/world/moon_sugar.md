# World Element

## What it is

Pale blue sugar harvested from frost that forms on the far side of the Moon.
It arrives once a month in small clay jars, packed in straw.

## Why it matters

It is what makes festival buns glow — and the Star Festival shipment is late,
which is the whole problem of the story.

## Sensory details

Cold on the tongue for one second, then warm. Sounds like faint sleigh bells
when poured. Glows faintly in a dark pantry, like a jar of low stars.

## Rules or constraints

- Buns glow only when moon-sugar meets the Moon Oven's heat; either alone
  does nothing.
- A jar holds enough for exactly one festival batch. No stretching it.

## Connected characters

Nova (bakes with it), Crumb (tracks the pantry inventory jar by jar).

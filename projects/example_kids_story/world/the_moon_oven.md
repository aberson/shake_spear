# World Element

## What it is

The bakery's oven: a round stone oven with a squeaky door, older than Crater
Lane itself. Grandmother always said it was here before the bakery was.

## Why it matters

It is the warm heart of the story — every important conversation happens in
front of it, and festival buns only glow when baked in it.

## Sensory details

Door squeaks on a rising note, like it's asking a question. Heat that smells
of toast and something faintly like rain. A hum you feel in your teeth when
it's ready.

## Rules or constraints

- It bakes at exactly one temperature: right.
- The door must be closed gently. Slam it and the whole batch sulks flat.

## Connected characters

Nova (bakes with it daily), Crumb (warms his mitt on its door).

## Connected scenes

the_quiet_oven, the_last_jar_of_moon_sugar

## Established facts

- Older than the settlement; origin unknown.
- Festival buns glow only if baked in this oven with moon-sugar.
